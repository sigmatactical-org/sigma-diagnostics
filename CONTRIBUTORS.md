## Upstream lineage

This project was originally published as **can-viewer** (Tauri + TypeScript). That work is gratefully acknowledged. The 0.3.0 release replaces the WebView frontend with a native Slint UI; the repository and crate are now named **diagnostics**.

Earlier standalone development of **dbc-rs** and related bounded collection work remains upstream lineage under MIT OR Apache-2.0.
